My camera and microphone doesn't work

Symptoms

  • You are using Brave or Firefox,
  • You can't activate the camera or the microphone,
  • You don't see or hear other participants in the video call,
  • But you can read the chat messages and write to it.

Diagnosis

It is possible that your browser has disabled WebRTC to avoid leaking your IP address.

Explanation

Your IP address is like your license plate. The web is like a city full of cameras. Through these cameras, a computer can detect the license plate of your car and know the places you visited and thus monitor you or show you personalized advertising. To avoid this, you can use a VPN, which is like a fake badge, and thus protect your privacy . However, some websites use WebRTC, an audio and video sharing technology. So, in some circumstances, allows you to reveal your true IP address. For more information, click here.

Is it safe to enable WebRTC?

  • Enabling WebRTC has no impact on your security, but it does have an impact on your privacy.
  • If you are using a VPN, enabling WebRTC can reveal your true IP address. To check, do this test.
  • If you are not using a VPN, enabling WebRTC will not change your privacy at all. However, just in case, do the test.

Solution

Brave

  1. In the address bar, type brave://settings/help to update Brave to the latest version.
  2. Go to coinosis.co.
  3. In the Brave bar, click Brave shields.
  4. Select Advanced View.
  5. Look for the lock option fingerprinting.
  6. Select All fingerprinting attempts allowed.
  7. Refresh the page.

Firefox

Note: This procedure enables WebRTC on all websites.
  1. In the address bar, type about:config.
  2. In the configuration options search field, type media.peerconnection.enabled.
  3. Change the value to true by clicking the button on the right.
  4. Close the configuration options tab.
  5. Refresh the page.
